kentucky burbon festival

Event Overview Experience the Bourbon Classic 2026, a premier celebration of bourbon heritage, creativity, and culinary excellence, hosted at the Kentucky International Convention Center on February 27–28, 2026. This immersive two-day event highlights the artistry behind bourbon making, featuring top distillers, expert blenders, and acclaimed chefs from across the industry.

Guests will enjoy exclusive tastings, insightful panel discussions, and hands-on workshops exploring bourbon's distinct flavors, cocktail design, and gourmet pairings. Whether you're a seasoned bourbon lover or just discovering the spirit, this event provides an exceptional chance to deepen your understanding and appreciation.

Savor chef-curated dishes inspired by bourbon, watch live mixology competitions, and meet the talented professionals shaping the future of whiskey. Connect with fellow enthusiasts and celebrate the spirit that defines Kentucky's legacy.

Don't miss this remarkable weekend devoted to bourbon culture, craftsmanship, and innovation. Reserve your spot for the Bourbon Classic 2026 and enjoy the ultimate experience of Kentucky tradition, flavor, and sophistication.



Kosair circus

Come one, come all, to the KOSAIR Circus, arriving at the Kentucky Exposition Center from January 30 through February 1, 2026! Prepare to be amazed by a spectacular showcase of high-flying acrobatics, daring stunts, and captivating performances that will keep you thrilled from start to finish. Watch fearless aerial performers soar above the crowd, laugh along with the antics of playful clowns, and marvel at the remarkable skill and grace of the show's talented animals.

This cherished family tradition brings endless excitement with its brilliant lights, upbeat music, and lively atmosphere that perfectly embodies the magic of the circus. Blending classic acts with modern flair, the KOSAIR Circus promises an unforgettable adventure for audiences of all ages.

Don't miss your chance to join the fun—grab your tickets today and step into a world filled with amazement, laughter, and pure entertainment!


National Farm Machinery Show


The National Farm Machinery Show 2026 returns to the Kentucky Exposition Center in Louisville from February 11–14, 2026, bringing together more than 800 exhibitors across 1.2 million square feet of indoor exhibit space. This premier agricultural event showcases the newest innovations in farming equipment, products, and services—all under one roof.

Attendees will have the chance to explore cutting-edge technology, participate in interactive demonstrations, and experience exclusive product unveilings designed for today's agricultural professionals.

One of the show's most anticipated attractions, the Championship Tractor Pull, will take place during select evening sessions and culminate with an exciting final round on Saturday at noon.

Join farmers, industry experts, and exhibitors from around the world for four days of innovation, networking, and high-powered competition at the National Farm Machinery Show 2026.
